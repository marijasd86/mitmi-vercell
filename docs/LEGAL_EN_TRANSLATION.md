## Privacy Policy

Version 1.0  
Effective date: April 1, 2026  
Last updated: April 1, 2026

### Short version

We collect only the data necessary for the platform to function. We do not sell your data. We do not show your precise location. You can request account deletion and data deletion at any time.

### 1. Who We Are

MITMI is a platform for finding company for events. The platform operator is **Marija Miladinović**, an individual, with contact address: **Rasadnička 14, 11300 Smederevo, Serbia**.

Privacy contact: [marijasd@gmail.com](mailto:marijasd@gmail.com)

### 2. What Data We Collect

**Data you provide:**

- name, username, and email address
- profile photo (optional)
- profile description and interests
- content you post, such as invites, messages, and, if activated in the future, ratings

**Data collected automatically:**

- approximate location (city or wider region, never a precise home address)
- device type and operating system
- time of platform use
- IP address

### 3. Why We Use Your Data

We use personal data for:

- account creation and management
- showing relevant events in your city
- enabling communication between users
- trust and community features and, if activated later, ratings
- platform safety and abuse prevention
- anonymous analytics and service improvement

### 4. Location

We use **only approximate location** (city or wider region) to show relevant events. We do not show your exact address to other users. Precise GPS location is not stored on our servers.

### 5. Data Sharing

We do **not sell** personal data to third parties. Data may be shared only with:

- technical service providers, such as hosting and email providers, only to the extent necessary to run the platform
- competent public authorities, only where required by law

### 6. Profile Visibility

Your public profile, including name, photo, description, and basic profile signals, is visible only to **registered users**. Non-registered visitors may see events, but not user profiles.

You may hide certain invites or parts of your activity using privacy controls inside the app.

### 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- access the data we process about you
- correct inaccurate data
- delete your account and personal data
- request a portable copy of your data
- object to certain processing activities
- withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis

Requests may be sent to [marijasd@gmail.com](mailto:marijasd@gmail.com). We aim to respond within 30 days.

### 8. Data Retention

We keep data while your account is active. After account deletion, personal data is deleted within 30 days, except where we are legally required to keep certain records longer.

Anonymous statistical data may be retained longer for analytics purposes.

### 9. Cookies

We use only necessary technical cookies required for the platform to function. We do not use advertising cookies for tracking-based ad targeting.

### 10. Children

The platform is intended only for persons over 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a minor created an account, we will remove it.

### 11. Security

We use standard security measures such as HTTPS, access control, and platform-level safeguards. However, no system is completely secure. Security concerns can be reported to [marijasd@gmail.com](mailto:marijasd@gmail.com).
