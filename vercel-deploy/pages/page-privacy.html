Kratko i jasno: Skupljamo samo podatke koji su neophodni za funkcionisanje platforme. Ne prodajemo vaše podatke. Ne prikazujemo vašu preciznu lokaciju. Možete obrisati nalog i sve podatke u svakom trenutku.

1. Ko smo mi

svita je platforma za pronalaženje društva za događaje. Operator platforme je Marija Miladinović, fizičko lice sa adresom za kontakt: Rasadnička 14, 11300 Smederevo, Srbija.

Kontakt za pitanja o privatnosti: marijasd@gmail.com

2. Koji podaci se prikupljaju

Podaci koje vi dajete:

  • Ime, korisničko ime, email adresa
  • Profilna fotografija (opcionalno)
  • Opis profila i interesovanja
  • Sadržaj koji objavljujete (planovi, poruke, ocene)

Podaci koji se automatski prikupljaju:

  • Okvirna lokacija (grad ili širi region — nikad precizna adresa)
  • Tip uređaja i operativni sistem
  • Vreme korišćenja platforme
  • IP adresa

3. Zašto koristimo vaše podatke

  • Kreiranje i upravljanje nalogom
  • Prikazivanje relevantnih događaja u vašem gradu
  • Omogućavanje komunikacije između korisnika
  • Izgradnja signala poverenja na platformi i, kada funkcija bude aktivna, sistem ocenjivanja
  • Bezbednost platforme i sprečavanje zloupotreba
  • Anonimna analitika za poboljšanje platforme

4. Lokacija

Koristimo isključivo okvirnu lokaciju (grad ili širi region) za prikazivanje relevantnih događaja. Nikad ne prikazujemo vašu tačnu adresu drugim korisnicima. Precizna GPS lokacija se ne čuva na serverima.

5. Deljenje podataka

Vaše lične podatke ne prodajemo trećim stranama. Podaci se mogu deliti sa:

  • Tehničkim servis provajderima (hosting, email servisi) — samo u meri neophodnoj za rad platforme
  • Nadležnim organima — isključivo na osnovu zakonske obaveze

6. Vidljivost profila

Vaš javni profil (ime, slika, opis i osnovni signali profila) vidljiv je samo registrovanim korisnicima. Neregistrovani posetioci mogu videti događaje, ali ne i profile korisnika.

Možete sakriti određene planove i delove aktivnosti sa svog profila kroz podešavanja privatnosti dostupna u aplikaciji.

7. Vaša prava (GDPR)

Imate pravo da:

  • Pristupite svim podacima koje čuvamo o vama
  • Ispravite netačne podatke
  • Obrišete nalog i sve lične podatke
  • Prenesete svoje podatke u mašinski čitljivom formatu
  • Prigovorite određenim načinima obrade
  • Povučete saglasnost u svakom trenutku

Većinu zahteva možeš pokrenuti direktno kroz aplikaciju, a za dodatna pitanja ili formalni zahtev piši na marijasd@gmail.com. Odgovaramo u roku od 30 dana.

8. Čuvanje podataka

Podatke čuvamo dok je nalog aktivan. Nakon brisanja naloga, podaci se trajno brišu u roku od 30 dana, osim podataka koje smo zakonski obavezni da čuvamo duže (npr. finansijski zapisi).

Anonimizovani statistički podaci (bez ličnih informacija) mogu se čuvati duže za analitičke svrhe.

9. Kolačići

Koristimo neophodne tehničke kolačiće za funkcionisanje platforme. Ne koristimo kolačiće za reklamno targetiranje.

10. Deca

Platforma je namenjena osobama starijim od 18 godina. Ne skupljamo namerno podatke od maloletnih lica. Ako saznamo da je maloletno lice kreiralo nalog, odmah ga brišemo.

11. Bezbednost

Koristimo industrijske standarde zaštite (HTTPS, enkripcija, kontrola pristupa). Uprkos tome, nijedan sistem nije 100% bezbedan. O bezbednosnim problemima obavestite nas na marijasd@gmail.com.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026.
In short: We collect only the data necessary for the platform to function. We do not sell your data. We do not show your precise location. You can request account deletion and data deletion at any time.

1. Who We Are

svita is a platform for finding company for events. The platform operator is Marija Miladinović, an individual, with contact address: Rasadnička 14, 11300 Smederevo, Serbia.

Privacy contact: marijasd@gmail.com

2. What Data We Collect

Data you provide:

  • name, username, and email address
  • profile photo (optional)
  • profile description and interests
  • content you post, such as plans, messages, and, if activated in the future, ratings

Data collected automatically:

  • approximate location (city or wider region, never a precise home address)
  • device type and operating system
  • time of platform use
  • IP address

3. Why We Use Your Data

  • account creation and management
  • showing relevant events in your city
  • enabling communication between users
  • trust and community features and, if activated later, ratings
  • platform safety and abuse prevention
  • anonymous analytics and service improvement

4. Location

We use only approximate location (city or wider region) to show relevant events. We do not show your exact address to other users. Precise GPS location is not stored on our servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data to third parties. Data may be shared only with:

  • technical service providers, such as hosting and email providers, only to the extent necessary to run the platform
  • competent public authorities, only where required by law

6. Profile Visibility

Your public profile, including name, photo, description, and basic profile signals, is visible only to registered users. Non-registered visitors may see events, but not user profiles.

You may hide certain plans or parts of your activity using privacy controls inside the app.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • access the data we process about you
  • correct inaccurate data
  • delete your account and personal data
  • request a portable copy of your data
  • object to certain processing activities
  • withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis

Most requests can be started directly inside the app. For additional questions or a formal request, email marijasd@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We keep data while your account is active. After account deletion, personal data is deleted within 30 days, except where we are legally required to keep certain records longer.

Anonymous statistical data may be retained longer for analytics purposes.

9. Cookies

We use only necessary technical cookies required for the platform to function. We do not use advertising cookies for tracking-based ad targeting.

10. Children

The platform is intended only for persons over 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we become aware that a minor created an account, we will remove it.

11. Security

We use standard security measures such as HTTPS, access control, and platform-level safeguards. However, no system is completely secure. Security concerns can be reported to marijasd@gmail.com.
